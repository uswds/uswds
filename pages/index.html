DraftTechFAR Hub

The TechFAR Hub provides rescources to apply the best practices to the world of Digital Service Acquisition across U.S. federal government

Discover Digital Service Acquisitions

Implementing a digital service acquisition correctly from the start has been demonstrated to greatly reduce cost and risk and improve end-user satisfaction. Before you embark on your acquisition you should read the Digital Service Playbook and The TechFar Handbook!

.

Not all training has to be done in the classroom! Specialized videos are a great rescource for discovering new acquisition strategies. Digital Service Acquisition Communities

As you move through your journey there will be questions, that is a good thing! In support of this we have created DITAP Chat to allow discussions amongst anyone interested in Digital Service Acquisitions. Coming soon we will also have a DITAP Blog which will allow members of the federal acquisition community to share their experiences.

DITAP Chat

DITAP Chat is the Digital IT Acquisition Professional platform on Stack Exchange that is open to discuss any questions that arise along the journey to improve the Federal Acquisition of Digital Services. It is still in the building phase, so help support the community by following and contributing to the dicussion!

How to Create A Digital Service Acquisition

Okay, before we start building your Request for Quote (RFQ), let's get an estimate. Currently we offer two methods. The first, App Estimator, will help you estimate the cost and rescources associated with building a single app. The Second, Agile Estimator will help you build an estimate for what we call "agile in a box" (i.e., an iterative development team).
